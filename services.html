OUR SERVICES

Content Producing

We are prepared to create content for everything from Short Film content to Music videos and original narrative content. We have Experience in working with small creators and big creators.

Producing

From Producing to Co-producing we are prepared to help you no matter what your planning a feature film, a documentary, or a short film if we like the concept, Blebsed Products are ready to help with all aspects of production from pre-production to post-production we want to be able to share our experience and knowledge and contacts we are prepared to work with all budgets and we are willing to match the production to your budget.

We work on all aspects of packaging of your film, website, social media, IMDb and all other elements.
